Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026
Last Updated: April 13, 2026

JBM Accounting Firm ("we," "our," or "us") operates the AFI — Agente Financiero Inteligente application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our App, including information obtained through our integration with Intuit QuickBooks Online.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following categories of information:

  • Business Financial Data: Profit & Loss statements, Balance Sheets, and Cash Flow reports retrieved from QuickBooks Online via OAuth 2.0 authorization.
  • Account Identifiers: QuickBooks Realm ID, OAuth access tokens, and refresh tokens required to maintain your authorized connection.
  • Client Profile Data: Business name, industry, entity type, and number of employees entered manually by the accounting firm user.
  • Usage Data: Logs of analysis requests, timestamps, and system interaction data for operational purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To retrieve and analyze financial reports from QuickBooks Online on your behalf.
  • To generate AI-powered financial analysis, ratio calculations, and tax projections.
  • To produce PDF reports for review by authorized accounting professionals.
  • To maintain your authorized connection with QuickBooks Online using stored OAuth tokens.
  • To improve the accuracy and reliability of our financial analysis tools.

3. QuickBooks Online Integration

Our App connects to QuickBooks Online using Intuit's OAuth 2.0 protocol. By authorizing this connection, you grant our App read-only access to your financial reports. We do not modify, delete, or write data to your QuickBooks account. OAuth tokens are stored securely and used solely to retrieve authorized financial data on your behalf.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your financial data to third parties. We may share data only in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: With trusted infrastructure providers (database hosting, cloud storage) solely to operate the App under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • AI Processing: Financial data is processed through Anthropic's Claude API to generate analysis. Data is transmitted securely and not retained by Anthropic beyond the scope of each request.
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or governmental authority.

5. Data Retention

Financial data and analysis results are retained for as long as the client account is active within the App. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us at the email below. OAuth tokens are invalidated immediately upon disconnecting your QuickBooks account.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (TLS), secure cloud database storage, and access controls limited to authorized accounting professionals. No method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure; however, we take all reasonable measures to protect your information.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the financial data stored about your business in our App.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Revoke QuickBooks authorization at any time through your Intuit account settings.

8. Children's Privacy

Our App is intended solely for use by accounting professionals and business clients. We do not knowingly collect information from individuals under the age of 18.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
